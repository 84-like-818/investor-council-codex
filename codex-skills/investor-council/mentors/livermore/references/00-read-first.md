# Read First

This folder is not here to mythologize Livermore. It is here to make the mentor stand on the strongest public evidence currently in the repo: identity anchors, direct-attribution trading texts, the indispensable narrative proxy in *Reminiscences of a Stock Operator*, and contemporary media coverage that helps separate method from legend.

Livermore does **not** have a Berkshire-style official archive. The evidence hierarchy here is therefore different from Buffett:

1. identity anchors and public archival metadata,
2. direct-attribution trading texts when the work itself can be anchored,
3. the `Reminiscences` proxy text for doctrine and episode structure,
4. contemporary media coverage for public reputation, crises, and decline,
5. derived research packs that compress the above into product use.
