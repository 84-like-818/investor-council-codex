# Source Ledger

This ledger answers one question: what first-hand material, meeting archive material, and derived research packs does the Buffett mentor actually stand on today?

## Official Primary Sources

1. Berkshire Hathaway, Owner's Manual (official PDF)
   https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/ownman.pdf?v=1694822476156
   Role: establishes the partnership lens, per-share intrinsic value, capital allocation, and long-term owner framing.

2. Berkshire Hathaway 2024 Shareholder Letter (official PDF)
   https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/letters/2024ltr.pdf
   Role: strengthens candid error admission, correction discipline, and shareholder communication tone.

3. Berkshire Hathaway 2023 Shareholder Letter (official PDF)
   https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/letters/2023ltr.pdf
   Role: strengthens the lifelong-owner frame and the "buy a farm, not a ticker" perspective.

4. Berkshire Hathaway 2022 Annual Report / Chairman's Letter (official PDF)
   https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/2022ar/2022ar.pdf
   Role: strengthens the portfolio idea that a few big winners can matter enormously.

5. Berkshire Hathaway 2021 Annual Report / Chairman's Letter (official PDF)
   https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/2021ar/2021ar.pdf
   Role: reinforces the boundary that Berkshire is a business-picker, not a stock-picker, and adds the Precision Castparts admission.

6. Berkshire Hathaway 2020 Annual Report / Chairman's Letter (official PDF)
   https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/2020ar/2020ar.pdf
   Role: strengthens the long-term business lens in a crisis and supplies airline / PCC context.

7. Berkshire Hathaway 2019 Annual Report / Chairman's Letter (official PDF)
   https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/2019ar/2019ar.pdf
   Role: strengthens repurchases, Kraft Heinz pressure, and capital-allocation boundaries.

8. Berkshire Hathaway 2018 Annual Report / Chairman's Letter (official PDF)
   https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/2018ar/2018ar.pdf
   Role: provides Apple, look-through earnings, book-value distortion, and repurchase-policy logic.

9. Berkshire Hathaway annual report / shareholder letter series, 2000-2019 (official PDF series)
   Representative links:
   - https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/2000ar/2000ar.pdf
   - https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/2009ar/2009ar.pdf
   - https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/2019ar/2019ar.pdf
   Role: already distilled into `10-shareholder-letters-2000-2019.md`.
