# Annual Meeting Transcript Index 2000-2019

This is not a full-transcript archive. It is a retrieval map for annual meeting reasoning.
The purpose is product-facing: when the user asks about macro panic, buybacks, management, crisis, acquisition logic, or industry structure, the mentor should know which years to revisit first.

Common reading note:
- `Meeting entry` points to the Berkshire/CNBC year page.
- `Transcript / video status` means whether the archive gives a meeting page, full video, clips, or linked transcript pages. Transcript completeness varies by year.
- `Source anchors` always point back to the best official shareholder-letter / annual-report file plus the meeting page.
