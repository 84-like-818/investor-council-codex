# Annual Meeting Q&A Themes 2000-2019

这份文件是年会 Q&A 主题索引。主要依据 Berkshire/CNBC 的 annual meeting archive 与年度 meeting page 摘要。
Archive: https://buffett.cnbc.com/annual-meetings/
它的价值在于：能让你快速知道每年 meeting 更像在回答哪一类问题。

## 2000
https://buffett.cnbc.com/2000-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/
- 互联网狂热更像财富转移，不是真正创造价值。

## 2001
https://buffett.cnbc.com/2001-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/
- 互联网零售对传统业务的威胁没有外界想的那么大。

## 2002
https://buffett.cnbc.com/2002-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/
- 9/11 之后保险业的逻辑如何改变。

## 2003
https://buffett.cnbc.com/2003-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/
- 衍生品为什么可能变成 financial weapons of mass destruction。

## 2004
https://buffett.cnbc.com/2004-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/

## 2005
https://buffett.cnbc.com/2005-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/
- 快速上涨的房价可能带来严重后果。

## 2006
https://buffett.cnbc.com/2006-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/

## 2007
https://buffett.cnbc.com/2007-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/

## 2008
https://buffett.cnbc.com/2008-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/

## 2009
https://buffett.cnbc.com/2009-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/

https://buffett.cnbc.com/2010-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/

https://buffett.cnbc.com/2011-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/

## 2012
https://buffett.cnbc.com/2012-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/

https://buffett.cnbc.com/2014-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/

https://buffett.cnbc.com/2017-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/

https://buffett.cnbc.com/2018-berkshire-hathaway-annual-meeting/

## 2019
