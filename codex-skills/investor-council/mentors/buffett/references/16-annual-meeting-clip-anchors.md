# Annual Meeting Clip Anchors

This file gives the product clip-level anchors instead of only meeting-level anchors.
Use it when the user wants Buffett's meeting-language on a specific topic, or when the answer needs a tighter transcript / clip handhold than the broader yearly meeting index.

- URL: https://buffett.cnbc.com/video/1994/05/07/buffett-on-usair-that-was-a-mistake-i-made.html
- Runtime / anchor: VIDEO 02:13
- Meeting context: 1994 Annual Meeting
- Why this clip matters: direct self-attribution of the USAir mistake.
- Best product use: airline-economics error, management vs industry economics, what Buffett sounds like when he owns an error.
